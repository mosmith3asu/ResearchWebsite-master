Applications

Manufacturing
Collaborative robotics allow for more direct interaction and joint-planning in assembly, inspection, and other tasks that may require online adpatation of the human-robot team. Thus, robots must be enabled with the ability to fluidly interact and jointly-plan in order to ensure efficiency and safety.
Search and Rescue
Search and rescue operations are a dynamic and high risk task that require tight-knit interaction and planning between teammates. Therefore, robotics partners must be able accurately predict and respond to humans.
Social Robotics
Social robots are autonomous agents that communicate and interact with humans in naturalistic ways. To enable this, robots must appropriatley consider social norms and have accurate models of human responses.
